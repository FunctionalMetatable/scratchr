ABOUT SCRATCHR
ScratchR is the web application that powers the Scratch Online Community
<http://scratch.mit.edu> ScratchR runs on 
Apache <http://httpd.apache.org>, CakePHP <http://cakephp.org>
and MySQL <http://mysql.com>
